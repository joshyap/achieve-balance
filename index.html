About us

Victoria Yackevicz

I am a San Antonio native who graduated from John Jay H.S. and received my Bachelors degree from UTSA.  I was in the US Army for 8 years and received my Masters of Counseling degree from Webster's University.  I have had the amazing opportunity to serve as Clinical Director for San Antonio's only child advocacy center and as a Regional Director for a treatment foster care agency.


I believe the challenges we have been through help us along the path of who we can become. We each have the power and ability to make the necessary changes to feel better or to be better. I will be there to help provide you the tools and support to navigate those ups and downs, regardless of the things that may feel out of control. I am a Certified TFCBT therapist on the National registry.I have years of experience working with children-adults who have experienced sexual abuse, physical abuse, PTSD, grief, emotional abuse, neglect, anxiety and depression.  I can help you navigate those intense feelings and help get you to a place of healing. 

Patricia Sanchez

I have had the pleasure of serving the Texas community for 9 years. I graduated with both Bachelors and Masters Degrees from University of Texas El Paso. I am. Bilingual Certified Trauma Focused Cognitive Behavioral Therapist. Worked with a variety of age groups and acuity levels at treatment centers, agency and private practice. Skilled at developing counseling relationships with clients to develop treatment plans, process and work towards a favorable prognosis. Experienced as being part of multidisciplinary team to ensure best practice that will provide best prognosis for clients. Trained by Yale School of Medicine to provide Child and Family Traumatic Stress intervention (CFTSI). Supervised team and headed the triage process for admissions and maintain workload assignments at Mental health treatment center. Coordinated psychiatric inpatient and outpatient services and complete admission process based on acuity and level of care.
